06
THE BIG DIFFERENCE

Branding vs Marketing vs Advertising

Three words everyone confuses. Let's settle this once and for all.

🎨 Branding 📈 Marketing 📺 Advertising
Goal Build trust & identity Generate leads & revenue Grab attention fast
Timeframe Long-term (years) Medium-term (months) Short-term (days)
Message "This is who we are" "This is what we offer" "Buy this NOW"
Metric Brand recall & loyalty Conversions & ROI Clicks & reach
Analogy Your personality 🧠 Your dating strategy 💌 Your pickup line 😏

Branding is WHY they trust you. Marketing is HOW they find you. Advertising is WHERE they see you. 🔥

07
FRAMEWORK

The 7-Step Bulletproof Brand Strategy

Follow this framework to build a brand that lasts.

1

🎯 Define Your Target Audience

Who exactly are you talking to? Age, pain points, dreams, platforms they use.

2

🔍 Analyse Your Competitors

What are they doing? Where are their gaps? That's your opportunity.

3

🏛️ Define Your Brand Pillars

Your mission, vision, values & personality — the DNA of your brand.

4

🎨 Create Your Visual Identity

Logo, colours, fonts, imagery — make it memorable and consistent.

5

✍️ Develop Key Messaging

Tagline, elevator pitch, brand voice — the words that define you.

6

🚀 Implement Across All Touchpoints

Website, social media, packaging, team — consistency is king.

7

📊 Evaluate & Adjust

What's working? What's not? Great brands evolve constantly.

"A strategy without execution is a daydream. Execution without strategy is a nightmare." ⚡

🎯 Interactive Activity

SWOT Your Own Brand

Pick YOUR brand (personal or business) and fill this out:

💪 Strengths

  • → What are you known for?
  • → What's your unfair advantage?
  • → What do people compliment you on?

😰 Weaknesses

  • → What do people NOT know about you?
  • → Where are you inconsistent?
  • → What skills are you missing?

🚀 Opportunities

  • → What trends can you ride?
  • → What gap can only YOU fill?
  • → What platform is underused?

⚠️ Threats

  • → Who's doing it better right now?
  • → What could damage your reputation?
  • → What market change scares you?
⏱️ 3 Minutes — Then Pair Up & Share!
